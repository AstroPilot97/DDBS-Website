Will blue skies remain, or will the storm take over?

A mod that rose from the ashes of another DDLC mod back in February 2018, Blue Skies has truly come into its own.

Established as a psychological, choice heavy take on the world of DDLC, this mod aims to flesh out each character far beyond their stereotypes - giving them real personalities, a peek into what they're like in school, their hopes and dreams, and so much more.

With a team of dedicated writers, poets, musicians and artists, Blue Skies aims to truly reinvent the world of DDLC, giving the player an insight never seen before. With a heavy inspiration from Katawa Shoujo, expect to be taken on a roller coaster of emotions with your Doki of choice - so buckle up!

Features

  • - 3 Routes (Sayori, Yuri and Natsuki)
  • - 15+ hours of Content
  • - Good and Bad Endings
  • - New Characters
  • - Seasonal Events
  • - Exclusive Backgrounds and CGs
  • - Original Soundtrack
  • - Dynamic Dialogue Box
